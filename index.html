Technical Details
Authentication is the process of verifying the identity of a user or device
to ensure only authorized individuals can access the private network we supply. Methods such as passwords or fingerprints are often used here.
Technical Details
Encryption is the process of converting your data into an undecipherable code, making it secure from unauthorized access. This process uses complex algorithms such as AES-256, to scramble data.
Technical Details
Our VPN then creates a secure “tunnel”, where the scrambled data is sent to a remote, anonymous VPN server.
Technical Details
The data is then decrypted by the VPN server and sent to your destination on the internet. This masks (hides) your IP address.
Technical Details
The website sends its response our VPN server, we send it to you through the tunnel, and now you have secure, private, and cheap access to the Internet!
